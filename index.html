90s Archives

We are creating a startup dedicated to preserving 90s websites because the early internet was a vibrant, chaotic, and wildly creative space that shaped digital culture as we know it today. Many of these sites handcrafted with pure HTML, neon backgrounds, quirky GIFs, and experimental designs are disappearing, taking a piece of history with them.
